Vehicle Tracker

GPS Tracking & Fleet Management

This is a best selling cloud based solution for tracking of vehicles and valuable assets. In a restrictive economy you need to be sure that your money is effectively and efficiently utilized. Globe Vehicle Tracking and fleet management can help you achieve this goal by giving detailed vehicle information online at your fingertips 24/7,365 days a year. This is the most economic and affordable solution because Malawi is the most price conscious market and value for money is the most important factor.

How It Works

Vehicle tracking system provides live GPS tracking capability that enables tracking up to minute-by-minute information and management of your vehicles, through the easy-to-use web interface which is the most convenient way to track your vehicles using computer or any mobile device at any time.

Core Features

  • Real Time / Live Tracking: See current location of your vehicle in real time on maps with great detail anytime and anywhere. We are completely on cloud based technology. This solution supports up to 10 seconds data updates.
  • SMS and Email Alerts: The system generates instant SMS and Email alerts in case of any abnormal activities like device tempering or over-speeding etc.
  • Stop your vehicle by SMS: You can immobilize or stop your vehicle by sending an SMS.
  • Mobile App for you: Now you can see your vehicle location on the go. We have mobile application for your favorite Android Phone and iPhone. Its also supports tablets as well.
  • Geofence and Landmarks: Create fencing and whenever your vehicle enter or leave this fence, our system push you alert messages.
  • History Log: GPS data of your vehicle is stored online for past 90 days. Do not worry!!! You can download these reports and keep them forever.
  • Fleet Information System: From vehicle location to fuel usage, idle time, engine diagnostic codes, and more, Fleet Management system provides the accurate, timely data you need to make smart fleet management decision.
  • Anywhere, Anytime Information: Access reports and vehicle information from desktop, laptop, smart phone or tablet.

Installation and Support

  • Our easy-to-install GPS device takes 30 minutes to set up
  • The design of the Globe GPS device makes it simple and easy to install:
    • Easy two-wire (power and ground) installation and set-up
    • Sleek profile that allows for more flexible and covert installation
    • No antenna wires needed — comes built-in with the device
  • 24/7 Call Centre – GIL has dedicated and highly trained personnel at your disposal that can deal with your vehicle tracking emergencies in a professional manner.
