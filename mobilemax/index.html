Mobilemax

MobileMax is a high speed mobile broadband 3G with WiFi enabled internet access technology. This solution is ideal for Home Users or Small businesses with a wide range of flexible broadband internet plans.

MobileMax offers seamless high speed internet connection ‘on the go’ and can be easily used between the comfort of your home and offices within the city limits of Limbe, Blantyre, Zomba, Mzuzu and Lilongwe.
