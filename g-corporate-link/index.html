CONNECT YOUR WORLD

G-CorporateLINK provides a highly secure foundation that connects your corporate headquarters, data centers, branch offices, small offices and mobile workers to each other, and to the applications they use to be productive. With G-CorporateLINK you can converge multiple networks into a Multi-protocol label switching (MPLS) enabled IP VPN that allows users to rely on a single network to add solutions such as enterprise voice capabilities, collaboration or cloud applications.

To facilitate the sharing of ideas and speed up decision making, you need to make the latest voice and video applications available so people can communicate with each other clearly and effectively, wherever they are.

To stay ahead of the curve, you need the right bandwidth to support innovative communication tools. With G-CorporateLINK, you’re able to communicate quickly and securely between different sites, over a single – and private – network.

PUT CRITICAL APPLICATIONS

G-CorporateLINK puts you in control: you get to decide which network traffic needs the fastest delivery so that it automatically gets the most bandwidth. So, critical applications, and those using voice and video, can take priority over data

You can also control network traffic between remote locations. This will help improve the way you use business applications to increase productivity.

IMPROVE REMOTE DISASTER RECOVERY

Having a secure, fast and reliable G-CorporateLINK means you can virtually reconnect remote sites to backup locations. Business continuity is vital, and the ability to maintain it between remote worksites means you’ll remain competitive and compliant.

KEEP TRACK OF BANDWIDTH PATTERNS

GIL provides monitoring tools to keep track of usage and trend reports, log service requests, and validate services to make sure you stay on top of your bandwidth needs.

DEDICATED ACCOUNT MANAGERS

With G-CorporateLINK you can enjoy a fully managed service with service level agreements in place to ensure availability. One point of contact (Account Manager) means less time worrying about keeping your network running and more time spent focused on running your business
