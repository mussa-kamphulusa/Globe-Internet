Globe Internet Ltd in Customer Appreciation Drive

Leading Internet Service and ICT services provider Globe Internet Limited on Mothers’ Day Eve Thursday 14th October 2021 treated its Blantyre-based customers to a cocktail at the prestigious Lotus Hotel by Serendib in Namiwawa. The event was themed ‘Client Appreciation” and the activities of the night aligned to the same. During this event, the company invited some of their guests coming from various players in the private sector, government, and non-governmental institutions to a night of interaction over a cocktail.

In his official remarks, Globe Internet Limited Chief Commercial Officer Elias Imaan said “tonight is special because when you heard our call, you decided to honor us with your presence and we have one message for you in whatever language you understand, Thank you, Zikomo, Sikomo, Yewo, Merci beaucoup, Zayithwa and I can go on and on just to appreciate your great support given to Globe Internet Limited in the year 2021 and before. 2020 and 2021 have been difficult years to live and run business minding the current impact of the covid-19 pandemic on the economy. We do not take your support for granted. You have kept us going. Thank you so much.”

MC Dorothy Kachitsa helps Client Mr. Mwagomba announces a Winner.

“We also wanted to use this event as a rare opportunity to hear some of the untold responses and/or feedback you have on our services and being a listening brand, we are ready to factor your shared input into our Product Development and Client Relationship Strategy going forward” he added.

Commenting on the sides, one of the guests Mr. Miller John Joshua, Business Development Manager for Reunion Insurance said “while Globe Internet Limited organized this event to thank us, we are rather more grateful that they think about the customer and going the extra mile to give us a treat.
It can’t go without credit.”

Another client who opted to speak on anonymity said that “for Globe Internet, it is very key that this marks just a beginning because as their market grows, they may not always meet all of us at once. So, if they can do one or two more with other clients, that would be amazing.”

The cocktail started at 6 pm and ran to around 9 pm with some clients leaving just after the official program at 8:30 pm. Three clients namely Robert M’bwana of Malawi Blood Transfusion Service, Ibrahim Mussa from Department of Immigration, and Lusekelo Mwagomba from Saint Andrews International walked away each with a lunch or dinner voucher for two at The Cinnamon Grill at Lotus Hotel following a raffle draw which featured all guest business cards.

In his closing remarks, Imaan said, “Our portfolio of services is comprehensive and cross-cutting to cater for all customer needs – Internet for corporates and individuals, MPLS Virtual Private Networks, Data Centre Cloud Services and Co-location, Managed Services, Vehicle Tracking, and Fleet Management, Generator Monitoring, Guard Monitoring, just to mention a few so you can clearly see that we are not self-made and we will continue to utilize every opportunity available for us to appreciate our clients.

Daniel Ngwira-Brand-Manager on the left, Mr. Mhlanga -MHC, and Mr.-Chawala IT Specialist (right).

Globe Internet Ltd (GIL) is a recognized leader in providing state-of-the-art Internet connectivity solutions and networking services with a wide spectrum of businesses enabling communication solutions through internet connectivity with the worldwide community at large.

Primarily established in 1999 as an Internet Service Provider, Globe Internet Limited has over the years evolved into a total ICT solutions provider for big Enterprise Businesses, Government Institutions, Non-governmental, Small and Medium Enterprises, and Individuals.
